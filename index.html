About Me

Hello! I'm Patrick Rolwes, a Work Psychologist with a passion for research and translating data into actionable insights. I know what you are thinking and you would be right! I am a total nerd. Over the last 4 years, I have been fortunate enough to have worked on a wide range of projects that have honed my research skills and taught me the true meaning of “Applied Research”. My research interests focus on selection/assessment development, job analysis, and competency development. I've had the opportunity to work in both the private and public sectors, currently serving as the Manager of Research and Development at a small consulting firm. In my current role, I support clients with data-driven insights, design custom assessments, and develop dashboards for easy interpretation of results. I have also played a key role in improving survey methodologies and developing critical assessment tools during my time with the United States Air Force. I hold a Master’s degree in I/O Psychology from St. Mary’s University and a Bachelor’s degree in Experimental Psychology from Franciscan University of Steubenville. When I'm not working, I enjoy board games, playing rugby, and spending time with my beautiful wife and kids. I currently live and work in Dallas, Texas. This website is my platform to share my ideas, projects, and valuable resources I've discovered along the way. Let's connect!
